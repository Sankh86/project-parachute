Project Parachute is a network of therapists providing free, flexible, and confidential tele-mental health services to COVID-19 frontline healthcare workers and their families.

Find a therapist Find Group Support

Our network of therapists offers repeating sessions for stress management during the COVID-19 crisis. You select a therapist and then our volunteers remain on stand-by to support you throughout the booking process.

Frontliners have gone to work for us.

At Project Parachute, we’re going to work for them.

We can’t prevent you from hitting the ground, but like a parachute we can soften your landing.
