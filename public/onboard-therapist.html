Who?

We are looking for local community and private practice therapists who would like to support medical staff in the hospitals.

How can you help?

If you have the ability to provide pro bono online therapy services, please complete the form below.

We will keep all responses confidential and only share your details once we will have a match with a healthcare employee.

We know that times of uncertainty and chronic stress can exacerbate existing mental health concerns. Please consider supporting this effort and helping those on the front lines of the COVID-19 pandemic.

Please reach out to projectparachute.eleos@gmail.com to learn about volunteer opportunities with Project Parachute!

Please discuss providing pro-bono therapy with your supervisor before signing up to help.

Weekly Availability

Your availability will be used to help frontliners find a therapist that best fits their schedule. You will reach out to the client to schedule an appointment after we forward you their information. You have two options for providing availability:

  • Set aside specific days and times. (i.e., Mondays 12-1 and Wednesdays 8-9) or,
  • List your general availability (i.e., office hours; Mondays 9 – 5, Tuesdays 4 - 8)
