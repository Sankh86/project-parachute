Privacy Policy

Protecting your private information is our priority. We only ask you to provide information that we need in order to deliver the services or information you have requested.

This statement of Privacy applies to Project-Parachute.org and Project Parachute and governs data collection and usage. For the purposes of this Privacy Policy, unless otherwise noted, all references to Project Parachute include Project-Parachute.org (the “Site”) and Project Parachute (also referred to by pronouns “We,” “Us,” and “Our”). By using the Project Parachute website, you consent to the data practices described in this statement.

Collection of your Personal Information

We do not collect any personal information about you unless you voluntarily provide it to us. However, you may be required to provide certain personal information to us when you elect to use certain services available on our Site. These may include (a) requesting a therapist on the Site; (b) requesting group support through the Site; (c) creating a therapist profile on the Site; or (d) requesting help through contact forms posted throughout the Site. To wit, we will use your information for, but not limited to, communicating with you in relation to services you have requested from us.

Use of your Personal Information

Project Parachute collects and uses your personal information to operate its website and deliver the services you have requested. [How the data is saved and stored].

Handling of your Personal Information

For users requesting therapists:

All personally identifiable information (e.g., names, emails or phone numbers) submitted through forms on our website are momentarily saved to a cloud database. This information is used to automatically populate an email to our Project Parachute Volunteers at projectparachute.eleos@gmail.com; after which it is promptly deleted. Our volunteers maintain this information in a HIPPA compliant database, Notion.

For users requesting group support:

All personally identifiable information (e.g., names, emails or phone numbers) submitted through forms on our website are momentarily saved to a cloud database. This information is used to automatically populate an email to the individual leading the group that you select; after which it is promptly deleted.

For therapists:

All personally identifiable information (e.g., names, emails or phone numbers) submitted through forms on our website are permanently saved to a cloud database. We use this information to populate the therapist profiles on our Site. Some of the information you provide is kept hidden from users (including your email and phone number as specified on our forms).

We make good faith, reasonable efforts to verify that each of our volunteers has received acceptable training in the handling of personally identifiable data (e.g., that all volunteers have completed HIPAA Compliance Training via NSF, NIH, or CITI). We make good faith, reasonable efforts to verify the name, contact details, and professional licensure of therapists in our directory.

Volunteers and therapists are prohibited from using your personal information, except to provide the services advertised on our Site. Volunteers and Therapists are required to maintain the confidentiality of your information.

Sharing Information with Third Parties

Project Parachute does not sell, rent or lease its clientele lists to third parties.

Limitations

Though we make every effort to preserve your privacy, we cannot guarantee your privacy. Further, we may disclose personal information when required by law or where we have a good-faith belief that such action is necessary to comply with a judicial proceeding, a court order or legal process served on us. We may also disclose personal information in cooperation with a law enforcement request.

Changes to this Statement

Project Parachute reserves the right to change this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you about significant changes in the way we treat personal information by sending a notice to the primary email address you provide us, by placing a prominent notice on our site, and/or by updating any privacy information on this page. Your continued use of the Site and/or Services available through this Site after such modifications will constitute your: (a) acknowledgment of the modified Privacy Policy; and (b) agreement to abide and be bound by that Policy.

Contact Information

Project Parachute welcomes your questions or comments regarding this Statement of Privacy. If you believe that Project Parachute has not adhered to this Statement, please contact us at projectparachute.eleos@gmail.com.
