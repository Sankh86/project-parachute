Find a Therapist

Please read the following information before proceeding and then click continue at the bottom of this page to access our directory.

Project Parachute connects COVID-19 frontline healthcare workers and their family members (currently limited to spouses, domestic partners, and children) with therapists in their state who have donated free tele-mental health services.

In a moment, you will have the ability to choose a therapist from our directory.

When you select a therapist through the Project Parachute website, a volunteer will reach out to that therapist on your behalf. You will then receive a confirmation email from Project Parachute. Within a few days, your therapist will contact you directly to set up an appointment.

Our volunteers remain on stand-by to assist you throughout the booking process.

If for any reason your booking falls through, we are here to help you find another provider.

I Agree to Terms of Service

Therapists have donated therapy time to you to say thank you for your hard work that supports us all. We know that this is a stressful time and that working with a therapist can help to manage your stress.

We can't keep anyone from hitting the ground but therapy, like a parachute, can soften your landing.

We define a healthcare frontliner as someone who is...

  • In a face-to-face healthcare setting where they may or may not have direct contact and/or care for someone with COVID-19. This includes healthcare workers and also administrative staff (e.g. check in), cleaners, cafeteria workers, etc.
  • In a face-to-face public health setting (with homeless individuals, at COVID testing centers) to promote screening, testing, and care for those with COVID-19.
  • Providing mental health services to the frontline as part of their job in a healthcare setting (not exclusively over telehealth).

Therapist Location

Due to licensing board regulations, therapists have to be licensed in the state where you are currently living in order to provide telehealth services to you.

Temporarily working on the frontlines away from home?

Select a therapist in the state where you will be physically present over the next few months. For example, if you plan to return home in 1-2 weeks, select a therapist in your home state. If you plan to remain in a state where you work for a month or longer, select a therapist in the state where you are currently located. When you return home, you may need to transition to a different therapist who is licensed in your home state. Please email us at projectparachute.eleos@gmail.com if you have any questions or concerns.

Therapist Availability

Therapists availability reflects their office hours or times they set aside for pro-bono work. Specific appointment times may not be available, and therapists may have additional availability that is not listed here. The therapist will contact you directly to confirm their availability and schedule an appointment..

Insurance

All therapists on our site have agreed to provide pro-bono services during this first phase of the COVID-19 pandemic. However, you may want to select a therapist that takes your insurance in case you want to continue seeing them long-term.
